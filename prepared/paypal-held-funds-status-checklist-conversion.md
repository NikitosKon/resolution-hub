# Conversion review: PayPal held-funds status checklist

## Conversion rationale

The page resolves a diagnostic problem: the reader must identify the exact PayPal status, affected object, registered market, and account-specific next check. A consultation is a reasonable next step only after that self-check is complete and the reader still cannot interpret the notice or organise the relevant, redacted facts. The CTA must therefore appear after the useful diagnostic content, not before it or in place of an official PayPal route.

The conversion layer offers a paid, one-to-one consultation within the confirmed Resolution Hub service scope. It does not suggest that contacting the owner changes PayPal's decision, accelerates review, releases funds, or provides access to the account.

**ELIGIBLE_AFTER_USEFUL_ANSWER**

## What the client receives

The owner may provide, depending on the agreed paid scope and the facts supplied:

- an individual consultation;
- practical recommendations;
- a case-specific action plan;
- document-preparation guidance when the platform's current notice calls for it;
- appeal or support-message drafting assistance when appropriate.

The owner may review redacted notices, screenshots, correspondence, and relevant account information, explain what they appear to show, identify missing information, and recommend legitimate evidence or documents. The owner does not access the client's account or submit material on the client's behalf.

Pricing is discussed individually in Telegram after the client briefly describes the situation and before paid work begins.

## Telegram handoff

The handoff should ask the reader to describe the visible issue briefly and to use the platform's authenticated route for any actual account action. Suggested handoff text:

> В первом сообщении кратко укажите платформу, точную формулировку статуса, что именно недоступно и рынок регистрации аккаунта. Если прикладываете материал, удалите личные и платёжные данные. Официальные действия выполняйте только в своём авторизованном аккаунте PayPal.

The handoff must not ask for passwords, authentication codes, recovery phrases, complete card details, or unnecessary identity documents. It must not invite the reader to bypass verification, use another identity/account, alter documents, or send anything to a non-official PayPal route.

## Privacy check

- Request only a brief description and the minimum relevant, redacted evidence.
- Accept redacted notices, screenshots, and correspondence only when needed to understand the status.
- Explicitly tell the reader not to send passwords, login or authentication codes, recovery phrases/codes, complete payment-card information, or unnecessary identity documents initially.
- Do not put notice text, account details, article context, or personal information in the Telegram URL.
- Keep platform submissions and account actions inside PayPal's authenticated official route.
- Do not imply that the owner stores, accesses, or secures the reader's account.

## Claims check

The CTA and handoff:

- do not guarantee account recovery, release of held funds, verification approval, appeal success, review outcome, processing time, or any other platform decision;
- do not claim PayPal affiliation, privileged access, escalation power, or control over a review;
- do not promise a faster response or a particular result;
- do not provide legal, tax, or financial advice;
- do not turn market-specific examples into a universal rule;
- do not add a document list that is absent from the reader's current account notice;
- keep PayPal's official route primary for account actions.
