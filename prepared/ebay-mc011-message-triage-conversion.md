## Conversion rationale

This guide helps the reader classify an authenticated eBay MC011 or related restriction message before taking action. It asks the reader to confirm the registered market, account context, affected function, exact wording, and named next step. It deliberately stops before document preparation, payout troubleshooting, appeal strategy, recovery predictions, or any universal eBay rule.

A paid one-to-one consultation is a natural secondary step only when the reader has checked the official account route but still cannot determine which facts are missing or which branch the notice describes. The useful diagnostic answer must remain complete before this invitation. The consultation cannot replace eBay's authenticated route or decide what eBay will do.

- an individual consultation about the supplied facts;
- practical recommendations for separating confirmed facts from open questions;
- a case-specific action plan;
- guidance on organising legitimate documents when an authenticated notice actually requests them;
