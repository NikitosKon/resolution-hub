# Owner questionnaire: PayPal held-funds status checklist

Purpose: collect one bounded, optional first-hand record for `paypal-held-funds-status-checklist`. The guide does not require a personal case for factual publication. Do not send passwords, authentication codes, recovery phrases, full card or bank details, identity documents, account or transaction IDs, exact balances, or unredacted support references.

## Required questions

None. Owner experience is not essential to the page's intended diagnostic value.

## Optional questions

1. Did you personally encounter an unfamiliar PayPal status such as `held`, `on hold`, `pending`, `reserve`, or `unavailable`? Answer `Yes` or `No`. If `No`, stop here.
2. What was the registered account market/country and account type? Give only the country/region and `personal` or `business`.
3. What was the approximate date or safe date range, and what exact redacted status or notice did PayPal display? State where it appeared (for example, transaction, balance, account banner, or Resolution Center/Resolution Centre).
4. Which object was visibly affected: one payment, part of the balance, the account, a dispute/reversal, or withdrawal? Describe only what the account showed.
5. What instruction or button did the authenticated account display? Record the route, not private links or identifiers.
6. What actions did you actually take, in order, and what observable status or message followed? Record sequence only; do not infer that an action caused the result.
7. What was the observed outcome, if any, and approximate duration? If the outcome is unknown, say so. Do not provide a promised or universal timing.
8. What evidence exists for each statement: redacted account screen, transaction record, Resolution Center/Resolution Centre notice, email, or other? Give a neutral internal location and mark each item `VERIFIED`, `PARTIALLY_VERIFIED`, or `UNVERIFIED`.
9. May a redacted screenshot be reviewed for possible publication? It must prove one exact state and remove identity, contact details, account/transaction IDs, balances, card/bank data, codes, and support references. State the approximate capture date.
10. Which exact facts may be published, which must remain private, and may the record be described only as one anonymous, market-specific example?
